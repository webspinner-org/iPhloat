# iPhloat — first-build summary

iPhloat went from empty repository to live patron-facing site on
2026-04-30. The site went up at iphloat.com that day with:

- The marketing surface — product pages, brand voice, imagery,
  the patron-facing copy that frames iPhloat as a brand.
- An invited-tester sign-in gate. Until iPhloat clears
  pre-launch, only allowlisted email addresses reach the live
  site.
- The brand-voice chat panel, "Ask iPhloat," on every page.
  The chat answers questions in the brand's voice with inline
  citations from the iPhloat corpus.

This first-build was the validation case for the Webspinner
*One Sentence Website* capability — the ability to take three
offerings from a brand owner (imagery, prose, and a single
sentence of intent) and produce a deployed, governed, AI-Native
site. iPhloat was hand-built end-to-end so that the agents
described in [`AGENTS.md`](AGENTS.md) could be specified by the
work, not by speculation.

For the brand and product story, see [`README.md`](README.md)
and [`INTENT.md`](INTENT.md). For how the site is built, see
[`AGENTS.md`](AGENTS.md). For the Webspinner ecosystem in plain
language, see [`HOW-WEBSPINNER-WORKS.md`](HOW-WEBSPINNER-WORKS.md).
