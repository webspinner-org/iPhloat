Features

  • Guaranteed face-up flotation. The only premium iPhone case engineered to float screen-up. Approximately 18 grams of positive buoyancy, perimeter-distributed chamber that pulls the back toward the water. Self-orients in about two seconds. Visible from a moving boat at 30 yards in chop.
  • Waterproof to IP68 / 10 meters. Tested to IEC 60529 IP68. Maintains full seal at 10 meters of depth for 30 minutes. The double-gasket system is rated for 1,000 open-and-close cycles before any measurable seal degradation.
  • Drop-rated to MIL-STD-810H, 8 feet. Independently tested on 26 face, edge, and corner drops onto plywood-over-concrete from 8 feet — 21 percent higher than the closest premium competitor’s published rating.
  • MagSafe compatible. Magnet array calibrated to within 0.5mm alignment of Apple’s spec. Wireless charging at full 15W. MagSafe wallets, mounts, and chargers attach cleanly through the back panel.
  • True-sound acoustics. Ported channels behind the speaker grilles preserve voice-frequency fidelity for calls and FaceTime. Music playback is recognizably iPhloat — slightly warmer than naked, but not muted or distorted.
  • 9H tempered-glass screen protector. Laminated to the front shell. Touch sensitivity matches a naked iPhone screen. Replaceable by the user in under 60 seconds via the front-frame release.
  • True clear back panel. Optical-grade polycarbonate, not frosted. Your iPhone’s color, the Apple logo, the MagSafe ring, and the camera deck are all visible.
  • High-visibility safety trim option. Three trim colors: Marine Navy (default), Bone White, and Hi-Vis Horizon — an orange band around the buoyancy chamber, detectable from approximately 60 yards in daylight.
  • Lanyard and Crux mount points. Two integrated attachment points (top corners) accept any standard wrist or neck lanyard. The bottom edge accepts a Crux-compatible tripod or GoPro mount adapter (sold separately).
  • Designed to be your everyday case. Light enough (38g empty), thin enough (11.2mm at the thickest point), and quiet enough (no rattle, no creak) to live on your phone full-time. The flotation is the feature you forget about — until the moment you don’t.
