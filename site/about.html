About iPhloat

Every product story begins with someone losing something they shouldn’t have lost. iPhloat is no different. The case exists because too many of us have stood on a dock, on a boat, on a paddleboard, on a pool deck, watching the small dark rectangle that holds our photographs and our calendars and our boarding passes drop into water that is just a little too deep to reach. The category had two answers, and neither of them was good enough. So we built a third.

iPhloat is a brand built on Webspinner, the AI-Native ecosystem for content, commerce, and customer engagement. The site you are reading right now — every paragraph, every product page, every support answer — is rendered through Webspinner Studio, served from Webspinner.Cloud, and stewarded by the Webspinner Foundation. We are one of a small number of brands building entirely on the Webspinner stack, and we believe it is the way commerce websites will be built in the AI-native era. Less templating. Less marketing copy laundered through committees. More direct conversation between the brand and the customer, mediated by intelligence rather than by hierarchy.

If you have questions about iPhloat the product, ask the case. If you have questions about Webspinner the platform, visit webspinner.com. If you have questions about the Foundation that stewards both, visit webspinner.org. We are happy you are here.
